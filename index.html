About the Artist

Lorcan is an 18-year-old from Leixlip, Ireland, who's been drawing since he could hold a pen. While art holds a special place in his heart, he's equally enthusiastic about inventing captivating board games designed to keep you entertained for days on end. Currently, Lorcan is immersed in animation studies at Ballyfermot College Dublin, where he's refining his skills to bring his imaginative concepts to life.

When he's not busy with his studies, you'll likely find him spending quality time with friends and family, brainstorming ideas for his next gaming venture, or sketching out new characters.
